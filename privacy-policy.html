Siva Group Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to SivaGroup's Manpower Registration Application. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you register and use our platform for job seekers and manpower services. By using our platform, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you register, we collect personal details such as your name, contact information, qualifications, experience, and any other relevant data needed for manpower services

Account Information: We collect information about your login credentials and any updates you provide while using the platform.

Job Preferences: Information regarding your job preferences, including job types, locations, and industries of interest.

3. How We Use Your Information

To Provide Services: Your personal information is used to facilitate job matching, skill assessments, and placement services.

Communication: We may use your contact information to send job offers, updates, and important notices regarding your account.

Improvement: We use data for improving and optimizing our services, understanding user preferences, and providing better job matching.

Legal Compliance: Your information may be used to comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. Information Sharing

With Employers: We share relevant personal information with potential employers to facilitate job placement and offers.

Service Providers: We may share your data with third-party service providers who help us operate the platform or deliver services.

Legal Obligations: We may disclose personal data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities.

5. Data Protection

We prioritize the protection of your personal information. SivaGroup implements reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
